About WSM

A Baltic roll-up platform for critical mineral assets.

Systematic consolidation of overlooked geology across three Baltic jurisdictions, with near-term mine restarts and long-term permit-portfolio value creation.

Western Strategic Minerals AB identifies, acquires and restarts mines and permitted deposits that have been mothballed, mismanaged or abandoned by owners who lacked the capital, regulatory positioning or operational capability to realise their geological value.

We acquire at valuations reflecting current neglected state, not geological potential. We apply operational overhaul, modern management and CRMA-aligned permitting to restart production and unlock value.

Our operating geography spans Sweden (Bergslagen, Västerbotten, Norrbotten), Poland and Finland — three jurisdictions with established mining law, EU regulatory alignment and proven critical-mineral endowments.

WSM is not an exploration-stage speculator. Every acquisition target has existing geological data, historical production records or confirmed mineralisation. We buy known geology at distressed prices, install competent management and restart operations.

Capabilities

Three pillars, one platform.

Systematic permit acquisition for long-term portfolio value, mine restarts for near-term cash flow, and tailings valorisation to extract additional value from legacy assets.

01 — Permit Factory

Systematic permit acquisition

Long-term value

Filing and acquiring exploration permits across established Baltic mining districts, targeting nickel, cobalt, tungsten, molybdenum, vanadium, titanium and PGMs. Every permit filed against verified geological data with confirmed mineralisation indicators.

02 — Mine Restarts

Operational overhaul and restart

Near-term revenue

Acquiring mothballed and underperforming mine operations, installing modern management and restarting production. Management overhaul is the value lever — not greenfield discovery. Revenue from mines with known reserves and existing infrastructure.

03 — Tailings Valorisation

Value from legacy waste

Bridge revenue

Acquiring rights to historical tailings deposits with recoverable critical-mineral content. Modern processing economics make previously uneconomic tailings commercially viable. Revenue from material already mined, crushed and concentrated.

Proprietary Intelligence

Data-driven permit acquisition.

WSM operates a proprietary geological intelligence platform built on live SGU data, satellite imagery and environmental overlays to systematically identify and evaluate acquisition targets.

  • 01
    Live permit monitoring — real-time tracking of granted, applied, expired and prohibition-period permits across all Swedish mining districts.
  • 02
    Multi-commodity targeting — filtering by Ni, Co, W, Mo, V, Ti, PGM across bedrock geology, borehole data and historical production records.
  • 03
    Environmental overlay — protected areas, reindeer herding zones, Sami consultation requirements and transport infrastructure mapped against every permit.
  • 04
    Satellite imagery — Sentinel-2 imagery with cloud filtering for ground-truth verification of site conditions and access roads.
  • 05
    AI viability analysis — automated economic scoring integrating commodity prices, logistics, permitting complexity and geological confidence.

What Separates Us

Consolidation thesis, operational restart, CRMA positioning.

Five things that materially distinguish WSM from a conventional junior explorer.

  1. Roll-up model, not single-asset risk

    WSM acquires a diversified portfolio of undervalued mineral assets across multiple jurisdictions and commodity classes. Portfolio construction de-risks the thesis.

  2. Mine restarts over greenfield exploration

    Every target has confirmed mineralisation through historical production or geological survey data. We restart known deposits with management overhaul — not speculative drilling.

  3. EU Critical Raw Materials Act alignment

    Operating exclusively within EU member states positions every asset for CRMA Strategic Project designation, unlocking accelerated permitting and co-financing eligibility.

  4. Proprietary geological intelligence platform

    Live SGU permit data, satellite imagery, environmental overlays and AI-assisted economic viability analysis. Systematic target identification at a pace manual prospecting cannot match.

  5. Three-jurisdiction Baltic footprint

    Sweden, Poland and Finland offer established mining law, EU regulatory alignment, skilled labour markets and infrastructure. Geographic diversification provides regulatory risk mitigation.

Regulatory Context

Positioned inside the EU CRMA framework.

The EU Critical Raw Materials Act (Regulation 2024/1252) establishes binding targets for domestic extraction, processing and recycling of critical and strategic raw materials within the European Union.

The Act creates a Strategic Projects designation granting accelerated permitting (24 months for extraction, 12 months for processing), streamlined environmental assessment and access to EU and member-state co-financing instruments.

WSM operates exclusively in EU member states and targets commodities on the CRMA lists. Every asset in the portfolio is eligible for Strategic Project designation, accelerated permitting and co-financing support that competitors outside the EU cannot access.
