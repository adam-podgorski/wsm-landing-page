Acquiring critical-mineral assets across the Baltic.

Western Strategic Minerals is an investment platform focused on the systematic consolidation of undervalued mineral operations and permitted deposits across Northern Europe.

Our Approach

Disciplined capital deployment into proven geology.

The firm identifies, acquires and restarts mines and permitted deposits that have been mothballed, mismanaged or overlooked. We acquire at valuations reflecting current neglected state—not geological potential—and apply operational overhaul to unlock value.

Every asset in the portfolio has existing geological data, historical production records or confirmed mineralisation. We deploy capital into known geology with management-driven restarts, not speculative exploration.

Core Strategies

A multi-strategy platform across the critical-minerals value chain.

The firm employs a diversified approach encompassing asset acquisition, operational restart and resource valorisation across multiple commodity classes and jurisdictions.

Permit Acquisition

Systematic filing and acquisition of exploration permits across established mining districts, building long-term portfolio value against verified geological data.

→

Operational Restarts

Acquiring mothballed mining operations and installing modern management to restart production from known reserves with existing infrastructure.

→

Tailings Valorisation

Acquiring rights to historical tailings deposits with recoverable critical-mineral content, where modern processing economics create commercial viability.

→

Regulatory Positioning

Operating exclusively within EU member states to position assets for accelerated permitting and co-financing under the Critical Raw Materials Act framework.

→

Commodity Diversification

Targeting a broad range of CRMA-listed commodities including battery metals, minor metals and platinum group metals to mitigate single-commodity risk.

→

Proprietary Intelligence

Data-driven target identification through a proprietary geological platform integrating live permit data, satellite imagery and AI-assisted economic analysis.

→
