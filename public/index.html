I · Baltic Critical Minerals

Acquiring and restarting underexploited mines across the Baltic.

A Swedish junior mining company consolidating dormant and underperforming critical-mineral operations across Sweden, Poland and Finland.

Western Strategic Minerals AB identifies, acquires and restarts mines and permitted deposits that have been mothballed, mismanaged or abandoned by owners who lacked the capital, regulatory positioning or operational capability to realise their geological value. We apply modern management, CRMA-aligned permitting strategy and systematic portfolio construction to unlock long-term value from known mineralisation.

II · Who We Are

A Baltic roll-up platform for critical mineral assets.

Western Strategic Minerals AB is a Swedish company built on a straightforward thesis: the Baltic region holds significant critical-mineral resources in mines and permit areas that are dormant, underperforming or controlled by owners who lack the capital, regulatory positioning or technical capability to develop them.

We acquire these assets at valuations that reflect their current neglected state, not their geological potential. We then apply operational overhaul, modern management and CRMA-aligned permitting to restart production and unlock value that previous owners could not.

Systematic consolidation of overlooked geology across three Baltic jurisdictions, with near-term mine restarts and long-term permit-portfolio value creation.

Our operating geography spans Sweden (Bergslagen, Västerbotten, Norrbotten), Poland and Finland — three jurisdictions with established mining law, EU regulatory alignment and proven critical-mineral endowments that remain structurally underexploited.

WSM is not an exploration-stage speculator. Every acquisition target has existing geological data, historical production records or confirmed mineralisation. We buy known geology at distressed prices, install competent management and restart operations.

Near-term revenue from material that has already been mined, crushed and concentrated.

IV · Proprietary Intelligence

Data-driven permit acquisition.

WSM operates a proprietary geological intelligence platform built on live SGU data, satellite imagery and environmental overlays to systematically identify and evaluate acquisition targets.

  • 01
    Live permit monitoring — real-time tracking of granted, applied, expired and prohibition-period permits across all Swedish mining districts via SGU OGC API integration.
  • 02
    Multi-commodity targeting — filtering by Ni, Co, W, Mo, V, Ti, PGM and other critical minerals across bedrock geology, borehole data and historical production records.
  • 03
    Environmental & infrastructure overlay — protected areas, reindeer herding zones, Sami consultation requirements and transport infrastructure mapped against every permit area.
  • 04
    Satellite imagery integration — Sentinel-2 imagery with cloud filtering for ground-truth verification of site conditions, access roads and active disturbance.
  • 05
    AI-assisted viability analysis — automated economic viability scoring integrating commodity prices, logistics costs, permitting complexity and geological confidence.

VII · What separates us

Consolidation thesis, operational restart, CRMA positioning.

Five things that materially distinguish WSM from a conventional junior explorer.

  1. Roll-up model, not single-asset risk

    WSM acquires a diversified portfolio of undervalued mineral assets across multiple jurisdictions and commodity classes. Portfolio construction de-risks the thesis: no single permit, mine or jurisdiction determines the outcome.

  2. Mine restarts over greenfield exploration

    Every acquisition target has confirmed mineralisation through historical production, geological survey data or prior exploration campaigns. We restart known deposits with management overhaul and operational improvement — not speculative drilling programmes.

  3. EU Critical Raw Materials Act alignment

    Operating exclusively within EU member states positions every asset for CRMA Strategic Project designation, unlocking accelerated permitting timelines, co-financing eligibility and priority regulatory treatment. Multiple applications filed or in preparation.

  4. Proprietary geological intelligence platform

    WSM’s proprietary data platform integrates live SGU permit data, satellite imagery, environmental overlays and AI-assisted economic viability analysis. Systematic identification and evaluation of acquisition targets at a pace and precision that manual prospecting cannot match.

  5. Three-jurisdiction Baltic footprint

    Sweden, Poland and Finland offer established mining law, EU regulatory alignment, skilled labour markets and infrastructure. Geographic diversification across three Baltic jurisdictions provides regulatory and political risk mitigation unavailable to single-country operators.

VIII · Regulatory Context

Positioned inside the EU CRMA framework.

The EU Critical Raw Materials Act (Regulation 2024/1252) establishes binding targets for domestic extraction, processing and recycling of critical and strategic raw materials within the European Union.

The Act creates a Strategic Projects designation that grants accelerated permitting (24 months for extraction, 12 months for processing), streamlined environmental assessment and access to EU and member-state co-financing instruments.

WSM operates exclusively in EU member states and targets commodities on the CRMA critical and strategic raw materials lists. This structural positioning means every asset in the WSM portfolio is eligible for Strategic Project designation, accelerated permitting and co-financing support that competitors outside the EU cannot access.
